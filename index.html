My Motivation

The first time I learned programming was when I was in Grade 4. I joined the robotic club and represented the school in an automatic robot competition at the Northeast Students Arts and Crafts Competition 2016. It was the first time I learned how to write code to control robots and had fun with programming from the IPST-MicroBox mechanical brain box set. In junior high school, I learned to program in basic Python language. I had opportunity to participate in The Promotion of Academic Olympiad and Development of Science Education Foundation under the patronage of Her Royal Highness Princess Galyani Vadhana Krom Luang Naradhiwas Rajanagarindra (POSN) Olympic Camp Computer, I learned about solving problems with coding. During the camp, I really enjoyed coding. Even though I wasn't selected as a representative of the national contest, I gained many new knowledge and experiences. From being just a computer user for working, using various convenience applications, or playing games, I realized that we could create new innovations by ourselves. I would like to develop myself in computers so that I can create something beneficial to society.
